Installing VPN-free.pro - Unlimited Secure VPN

Do not close this page until the installation is complete

While the extension is being installed, please read the user manual

Pin the extension

To quickly access the VPN, pin the extension to the toolbar.

1. Click on the puzzle piece icon in the upper right corner of your browser.
2. Locate the VPN-free.pro - Free Unlimited VPN extension in the list of extensions and pin it.

After pinning the extension, its action button will remain in the browser toolbar.

Select a country

Choose a country from the dropdown list of the VPN extension.

1. Click on the VPN-free.pro extension icon on the toolbar to access the extension menu.
2. Select a VPN server from the available countries in the dropdown menu.

After selecting the VPN country, it will automatically connect to the server you've chosen.

Turn off VPN

Instantly turn off the VPN if you don't need it right now.

1. Click on the VPN-free.pro extension icon on the toolbar to access the extension menu.
2. Select Turn off VPN option from the dropdown menu.

After disabling the VPN, all your traffic will go directly.
When the VPN is turned off, the icon will be gray.

To reconnect, simply select a country from the list, and the extension will automatically connect to the server you've chosen.

Support

Support is always ready to assist you.

1. Click on the VPN-free.pro extension icon on the toolbar to access the extension menu.
2. Click on the email address to send a request to customer support.

You can also send an email directly to our mailbox from any of your email addresses (Gmail, Hotmail, Yandex, and others).
We do not respond to questions asked in the reviews section of the Chrome Web Store.
