How to Set Up Secure DNS in Yandex Browser

  1. Click the or enter browser://protect into your browser's address bar.

    You can also open the above page manually: Menu → Settings → Security.

  2. Scroll down to the Secure connection section.

    Enable the Use a secure DNS server.

    Expand the options next to Use, and select Google (Public DNS) or Cloudflare (1.1.1.1).

  3. Be sure to restart your browser.
