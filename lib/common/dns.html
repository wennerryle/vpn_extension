How to Set Up Secure DNS in Google Chrome

  1. Click the or enter chrome://settings/security into your browser's address bar.

    You can also open the above page manually: Menu → Settings → Privacy and security → Security.

  2. Scroll down to the Advanced section.

    Enable the Encrypt the names of sites that you visit.

    Expand the options next to Select DNS provider, and select Google (Public DNS) or Cloudflare (1.1.1.1).

  3. Be sure to restart your browser.
